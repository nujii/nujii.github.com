Download Nujii Studio

Collaborate with friends. With your existing tools.

You've already got your DAW and all of your virtual instruments and effects. Nujii Studio adds to all of those by providing a common interface for collaborating across popular programs such as Pro Tools, Logic Pro, and Reaper

Revision control. Never lose a change.

Every single change you make gets stored so it can easily be reverted to at anytime. You can now do side-by-side comparisons of your tracks to make your next big hit.

File backup. Because your music is important.

Now you can sleep at night knowing that your music is stored with Nujii. Everything is encrypted meaning it's also secure. You'll never worry about a corrupted harddrive again.
