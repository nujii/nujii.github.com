Musicians do it together.

Real-Time Collaboration.

Nujii Studio lets you record your music with your friends anytime, anywhere. Simply open up Nujii Studio, add your friend as a collaborator, and get to work. While they're laying down a new guitar riff, you're in the mixer view EQ'ing the drums. All in real time.

Full Featured DAW.

Nujii studio is a full-featured DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) that gives you complete control over your music. With a full mixing board, piano roll, and track view, you have all the tools you need to make and mix your perfect album. And it’s open source, so you can make it yours. Go ahead and amaze us.

Instant Sharing.

Nujii Studio links directly to your favorite social network, so your friends and fans can follow along as you record your next hit. With one click, instantly share your creation with the world and get immediate feedback. No more cassette decks.

Get the app.

Nujii Studio is available as a free native app for Windows, Mac, and Linux.
